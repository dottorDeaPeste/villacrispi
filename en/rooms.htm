ROOMS AND RATES

The 9 rooms at Villa Crispi, with elegant tasteful furniture and modern carefully selected comforts, comparable to a 3 star hotel in Venice Mestre, guarantee guests the most pleasant stays for pleasure or business.
In a classical style, with beds and furnishings in natural or shellacked wood, with original design inspired by the 17th and 18th century French style. The hues are clear and relaxing, enhanced by parquet and rugs which make them particularly inviting.
These bright rooms are available in twins, doubles, triples and doubles for single use. All with private bathroom (with customized Villa Crispi beauty kit) with shower or tub, towel warmer and hairdryer, for a truly comfortable stay they have an individually adjustable air conditioner, satellite TV, direct dial phone, minibar, safe and Internet connection.
Breakfast can be served in the room for guests who desire this service.
